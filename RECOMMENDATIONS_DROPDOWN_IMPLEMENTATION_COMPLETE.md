## 🔧 **How It Works:**

### **Selection Method:**
1. **Click Field**: Click on the Recommendations field
2. **Start Typing**: Begin typing (e.g., "follow")
3. **See Suggestions**: Matching options appear in dropdown
4. **Select**: Click any suggestion to auto-fill
5. **Or Continue**: Keep typing your custom recommendation

### **Custom Entry Method:**
1. **Type Custom Text**: Enter any recommendation not in the list
2. **Visual Indicator**: See green "✨ New entry - will be saved for future use"
3. **Submit Form**: Create the medical certificate
4. **Auto-Save**: Custom recommendation is saved for future use
5. **Success Message**: Get confirmation that it was saved
